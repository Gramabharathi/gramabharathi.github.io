About Us

Self Sustained Village Development


Grama Bharathi is a registered charitable trust (NGO) based in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh States having presence in almost every district of these two states. Currently 60 villages are undergoing the village development programs. 30 villages have got their Village Development Associations which has memgers from every community of the village. The youth of the villages have visited Raleganasidhi, Chitrakoot and Mohaadh villages and studied the best practices in Rural Development.

Important focus areas are livelihood, health care, education and culture. Grama Bharathi was successful in implementing government scheme Gudi-Badi (temple-school) project in Gurrevula village in Warangal district.

Natural manure (Cow dung) based farming practices are being followed. 5000 farmers from about 82 villages have been trained regarding the natural manure based farming practices. In April 2012, about 800 farmers have been trained under Sri Subhash Palekar. Cleaning Villages under various seva programs and planting more than 2,50,000 in 100 schools with the help of Council for Green Revolution. Training and seminars for appointed members are provided by Grama Bharthi for effective implementation of their programs.
